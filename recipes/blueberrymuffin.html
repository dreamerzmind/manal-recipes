Blueberry Muffin

Ingredients

For the cake (donuts or cupcakes)

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups blueberries
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour (for tossing the bleberries in)
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 cup granukated white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup whole milk or milk substitute

For the Blueberry Glaze

  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 2 tablespoon blueberry jam
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2-3 tablespoon milk
