Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 2 to 3 medium ripe bananas
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 3/4 cup granulated or light brown sugar or dark brown sugar
  • 1 large egg white
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

Description

This Banana Bread will be the best addition to your breakfast and after dinner cravings.

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Melt the butter
  3. Whisk together the flour, baking soda, salt to a bowl.
  4. Whisk together the butter, sugar, egg, vanilla extract, and mashed bananas.
  5. Whisk the wet and dry ingredients together.
